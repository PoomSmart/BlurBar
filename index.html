BlurBar

Blur the iOS 7 statusbar on your homescreen. Uses private API exposed in CKBlurView. Special thanks to the many developers and friends who helped along the way.

Available for download on the BigBoss repository, or here.

BlurBar is completely open source and free. If you want to use the code, make sure to follow the license below, and feel free to e-mail me about any problems. Enjoy!
